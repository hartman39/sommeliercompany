We serve all five boroughs and surrounding areas including Westchester, Long Island, and Northern New Jersey. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond 25 miles.

Our New York team includes certified sommeliers with experience at Michelin-starred restaurants, extensive knowledge of global wines, and exceptional presentation skills.

Yes! We offer quarterly and annual corporate packages with preferred pricing, priority booking, and customized programs for regular team building and client entertainment.
